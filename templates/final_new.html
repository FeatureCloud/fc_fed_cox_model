Regression Coefficients

A positive sign means that the risk of the event is higher, and thus the prognosis worse, for individuals with higher values of that variable. A negative sign means lower risk of the event occuring.

Hazard Ratios

Hazard ratios are the exponentiated coefficients. A higher hazard ratio means more at risk of the event.

P-values

When the p-value is large, the results in the data are explainable by chance alone. A sufficiently small p-value, the results are statistically significant.

Concordance Index

The concordance index is a measure of the predicitive accuracy of the fitted model onto the training set. It is a value between 0 and 1, with 1 representing perfect concordance.
